About NYUAD's Campus

Every university campus has its own story to tell through its unique design and history. While academics are at the heart of a student’s journey, it is just as important to have welcoming spaces beyond the forms where students can relax, recharge, and find balance amid the demands of university life.

What are the Comfort Zones?

Today we invite you to explore the most popular “Comfort Zones” in campus—well-loved spaces where students can relax, connect with friends, enjoy games, focus on their studies, or share a meal.

"Everyone has a happy place; a place where they feel comfortable. NYUAD provides a place that fits everyone"

- NYUAD student
